About Our Club

Founded in 2010, the NYP Photography Club has been nurturing photography enthusiasts and professionals for over a decade. We are a community of passionate photographers dedicated to learning, sharing, and growing together.

Our Mission

Learn

Provide comprehensive photography education through workshops, mentorship, and hands-on experience.

Connect

Build a supportive community where photographers can share knowledge, experiences, and inspiration.

Excel

Help members develop their skills and achieve their full potential in photography.
